Heaven and Hell — Composition Study

Composition study for a 18x24 icon painting

Medium
Acrylic on Canvas
Year
2023
Format
16"x20"

Intent

The goal was to lock composition and gesture before committing to the large format. These studies test spacing, symmetry vs. natural movement, and the read of focal areas.

Process

  • Thumbnail value sketches
  • Gesture pass to establish posture and rhythm
  • Block-in and grouping adjustments
  • Refinement for clarity at mural scale
